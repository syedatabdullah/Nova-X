Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enroll in a course?

Simply browse our courses, select the one you're interested in, and click the "Enroll Now" button. Follow the registration process to get started.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and mobile wallets like EasyPaisa and JazzCash.

Are the courses self-paced?

While our courses follow a structured timeline, you have flexibility in completing the assignments and watching recorded lectures at your convenience.

Do you offer job placement?

Yes, we provide job placement assistance to our graduates through our network of partner companies and our dedicated career services team.
