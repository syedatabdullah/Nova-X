Amazon FBA Specialist

E-commerce Solutions Ltd Lahore, Pakistan Full Time

Job Description

We are seeking an experienced Amazon FBA Specialist to join our growing e-commerce team. The ideal candidate will have a proven track record in managing and scaling Amazon FBA businesses.

Key Responsibilities

  • Manage and optimize product listings across multiple Amazon marketplaces
  • Conduct market research and identify new product opportunities
  • Develop and implement PPC advertising strategies
  • Monitor and maintain inventory levels across warehouses
  • Handle supplier communications and negotiations
  • Analyze sales data and prepare performance reports

Requirements

  • 2+ years experience in Amazon FBA business management
  • Proven track record of increasing sales and profitability
  • Strong understanding of Amazon's algorithms and best practices
  • Experience with inventory management and forecasting
  • Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Strong communication skills in English
